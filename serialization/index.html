Snapshot Persistence

Serialization

A snapshot is a plain object: { value, context, event }. serializeSnapshot() makes it JSON-safe so you can store it anywhere — localStorage, a database, or send it over the wire. deserializeSnapshot() validates the shape and reconstructs it. Increment the counter, save, reset to zero, then load to resume exactly where you left off.
